About Us

Who We Are

Paaavarf is a non-governmental and not-for-profit organization that work to reduce and minimize the negative impact of violance and rape in our global community.

We strongly believe that the best way to achieve lasting peace is to make sure people get involved in providing alternatives to violence and have access to fair and effective means of addressing injustice and inequalities that drive conflict.

We have continually been working majorly at North Central States and we're based in Jos, Plateau State.

Read More

Mission and Vision

  • We work to promote conditions where people get actively involved in reducing and minimizing violence in their respective communities and build peace by providing alternatives to violence.
  • To join our hands together with other regulatory bodies against rape to protect women and female children against rape and ensure that any rapist caught in the act is fully dealt with.
  • We work to create conditions where people have access to fair and effective means of addressing injustice and inequalities that drive conflict.
